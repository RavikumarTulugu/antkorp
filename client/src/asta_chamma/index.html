Asta Chamma

We are resurrecting an age old game from south india, Asta Chamma. also called as Katte Mane (Kannada), Daayam (Tamil), Kaana Duaa (Hindi) Kavidi Kali (Malayalam).

How to play:

Your friends can be any where on the planet. Location doesn't matter. Login to the site with the facebook id.

You will be presented with a list of waiting boards. Boards where players are waiting for company. Join any of these and start playing. or Create a new board and invite your facebook friends to play with.

Game rules:

There are different variants of this game played with different rules. we selected the simplest version.

Pawn start:

Every player will have 4 pawns. Game starts with all the pawns in the player's home cell. The pawns cannot move from home cell until the dice rolls '4' or '8', after which the pawns move according to the roll count.

Turn:

If a player rolls 4 or 8 or made a kill, he will get another chance to roll the dice.

Killing other player's pawns:

If the pawn moves in to the cell in which there is already a pawn of other player, other player's pawn gets killed.

To resurrect the pawn the roll should be '4' or '8'. If the roll is '4' player can get a died pawn back, if it's 8 he can get 2 pawns back. To move the pawn or restore a died pawn is player's choice. Killing a pawn is not possible in a home cell.

Game over:

The player all of whose pawns reach the central home is the winner.
